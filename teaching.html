Teaching

Experiential learning

Field work is important for developing hypotheses... Some of my most formative experiences have been in the field... Seeing, hearing, and sensing the ecological world is the way to develop hypotheses. I have shared this training with many mentees in the field. My field site in Vita is accessible and provides opportunities for all people of all abilities to try out field work.
